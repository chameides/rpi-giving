Make an Impact

Philanthropy and strategic investments helped put into place the people, program, platforms, and partnerships that have built the global reputation of Rensselaer under The Rensselaer Plan. As we plan for the bicentennial of Rensselaer in 2024, we must continue to support these endeavors to become transformative in the global impact of our research, our innovative pedagogy, and in the lives of our students.

Empower Our People

Help us attract and retain the brightest students and professors who are seeking answers to today’s most pressing global challenges.

Provide For Our Platforms

Our collaborative research spaces encourage exploration and strengthen interdisciplinary inquiry, technological innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Fuel Our Programs

Through a series of strategic learning opportunities, our programs prepare the next generation of leaders to change the world.

Facilitate our Partnerships

Rensselaer donors, alumni, friends, corporations, and foundations help to elevate our global reach and impact.
